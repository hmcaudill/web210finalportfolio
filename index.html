About Me

Hello! My name is Heather Caudill and I'm currently a student at Wake Tech Community College. I specialize in web design, graphic design, and art in a multitude of different mediums. I have currently worked with:

  • The Daily Tar Heel at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where I specialized in page layouts and infographics.
  • Various art methods at my time spent at UNC, including painting, pencil, chalk, pastel, screen printing, pottery, and metal sculpture.
  • Various web design and coding formats, including CSS, HTML, JavaScript, PHP, and Bootstrap
  • Freelance Pet Portraits for those who want to commemorate their furry friends.
